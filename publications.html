Instant Heat Maps in R: How-To

We are living in the information age where huge amounts of data are readily available to anyone. However, not everyone knows how to make best use of it, yet.
In my book I give you a practical hands-on approach how to create heat maps using the free and probably most popular Statistical Software Package R! Don't worry, I already did the hard work for you and created all the code you need to create great heat maps from your data.
Detailed information on each approach make this book a valuable experience for beginners as well as experienced users of R.

So let us dive in and create ...
  • practical
  • meaningful
  • and compelling
Heat Maps!  
 
- Sebastian Raschka
