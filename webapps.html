DNA and Protein Sequence Cleaver

Splits a sequence into smaller chunks of user-defined length, and can extract sub-sequences given a start and end position.
  • supports FASTA format
  • removes whitespaces
  • works with all sorts of input sequences
  • variable start position for splitting mode

COMPLEMENT REVERSE tool for DNA and RNA sequences

Converts DNA or RNA sequence to its complementary, reversed, or complementary-reversed sequence.
  • removes non-DNA/RNA characters
  • automatically detects RNA sequences
  • supports FASTA headers

PDB to FASTA converter

Converts the contents of a PDB file into an 1-letter amino acid code.
  • creates FASTA headers
  • recognizes presence of multiple chains
  • tolerates ANISOU entries and multiple-occupancies
  • recognizes and converts DNA and RNA sequences
  • converts seleno-cysteine and seleno-methionine
  • also works for "incomplete" PDB files

PDB Center of Mass Calculator

Calculates the center of mass of protein and/or ligand atoms
  • center of mass is weighted by atom types
  • supports files in PDB format
  • tolerates ANISOU entries
  • also works for "incomplete" PDB files

Basic Statistics Calculator

Tool that calculates various kinds of statistics.
  • accepts data in (almost) any format
  • calculates N, sum, mode, mean, median, variance, standard deviation, standard error, and confidence intervals
  • converts dataset into csv format
